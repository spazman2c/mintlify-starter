## Channable Product Feed 

Product catalog feed integration with Channable , a marketplace aggregation platform. Used by Renewtech . Pushes the full IQ Reseller product catalog (items, pricing tiers, stock levels, categories) to Channable for distribution to downstream marketplaces.

### Catalog Feed Structure 

The catalog query ( Uri_FullCatalog ) joins maxlist_items with item to produce a flat feed containing: 

 | Field | Source 
 | id | Composite key: UPPER(CONCAT(mfgr, '~~', itemnumber, '~~', conditioncode)) 
 | brand | mfgr 
 | title / item_group_id | itemnumber 
 | condition | conditioncode 
 | description | item description 
 | price | item price 
 | sale_price1–8 | 8 tier pricing from item table 
 | stock | available qty 
 | product_type | Concatenated category hierarchy (up to 4 levels) 
 | image_link | item imageurl 

### Invocation 
IqSync to-channable catalog &lt;tenantID&gt; production 
Uses GetDataFromVendor_Channable() handler with PopulateCheetSheetFromDb() (settings loaded from database, not App.config).
